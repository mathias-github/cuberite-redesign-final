We understand that problems may occur, and we are happy to help you. This is how you can receive help with your problem.

Forum

The main way to receive help is by creating a thread in our forum. Community and team members of Cuberite will provide help when they have time.

Visit Forum

E-mail

If you need to contact the Cuberite team directly, you can use our e-mail address. Contact us in case of urgent issues, such as security problems, and PR reasons.
