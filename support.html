We understand that problems may occur, and we are happy to help you. This is how you can contact us and get help with your issue.

Forum

The best way to get help is by creating a topic in our forum. You may not get an answer instantly, but our members can provide help.

Getting Started

E-mail

If you need to contact the Cuberite team directly, you can use our e-mail address. Use this contact method in case of urgent issues, such as security problems, and PR reasons.

Send E-mail

IRC

Our IRC (Internet Relay Chat) channel is a faster method of receiving help, if you don't feel like signing up on our forum and creating a topic. Usually people are around to help you, but there might not always be someone online.
