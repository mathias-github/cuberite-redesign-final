Analytics

We use an analytics system to collect anonymous information about visitors on our websites. This information is only used by us to get a complete picture of activity, as well as to improve our services. If you don't want to be a part of our analytics system, you can opt-out by visiting this link.

Cookies

Whenever you visit our websites, we store small pieces of information on your computer called cookies. These cookies are part of our analytics system, and are used to to collect more accurate, but anonymous information about our visitors, including from where they were referred, and which pages they visit. Cookies are also used on our forum, where they serve an important role in keeping the forum functional. If you wish to disable cookies, you can do so in the settings of your web browser.
